We are deeply saddened by the sudden loss of Jeff, who—along with Phil—founded the HAT Run 50K in 1989 out of their shared love for trail running, at a time when ultra trail races were rare on the East Coast. Jeff's vision and commitment brought to life an event that has united runners for decades and built a vibrant community. As an accomplished ultrarunner, he not only inspired others with his achievements, but also with his humor, infectious laugh, and unwavering encouragement—especially at the finish line, where he cheered every participant. We are forever grateful for the legacy Jeff leaves behind, and we honor his memory with heartfelt thanks for the enduring gift of the HAT Run 50K. He will be deeply missed and fondly remembered by all who had the privilege to know him.
